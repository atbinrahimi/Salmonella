How Did They Use Money to Win the American Revolutionary War?

What did they use the money for?

During the Revolutionary War, the U.S. spent money to pay soldiers, buy food, weapons, and clothes, build ships, move troops and supplies, get help from other countries, and pay back loans from France and the Netherlands.
